Neurologist in Chisinau: when it makes sense to book a consultation

A simple guide for people with headaches, dizziness, numbness, back pain, poor sleep or body tension who are looking for a neurologist in Chisinau.

When you search for “neurologist Chisinau”, you are probably not just looking for a phone number. You are trying to understand what is happening in your body.

You may have headaches. You may feel dizzy. You may have back pain, numbness in your hands or legs, poor sleep, body tension or constant uneasiness. Sometimes the symptoms are clear. Sometimes they are confusing and you do not know where to start.

A neurological consultation can help put things in order. The doctor listens to your symptoms, checks reflexes, sensation, movement and balance, then decides what steps make sense next.

When should you see a neurologist?

It is worth booking a consultation if you have repeated headaches, dizziness, tingling, numbness, neck pain, lower back pain, weakness in an arm or leg, tremor, balance problems or very poor sleep.

It does not automatically mean something serious. But if symptoms repeat or affect daily life, it is better to check them than to ignore them.

Treatment should not be the same for everyone

Revimed PLUS+ is a clinic founded in 2005, with experience in neurology, rehabilitation and physiotherapy. Our doctor has more than 35 years of experience in the field, and treatment is selected individually for each patient.

This means we do not use the same plan for everyone. One patient may benefit from physiotherapy and gentler methods. Another patient may need medication. Sometimes the best option is a combination.

Everything is discussed with the patient. If you prefer a gentler approach and the situation allows it, the doctor can take that into account. If classic medical treatment is needed, you will be told why.

Gentler methods or medication?

Many patients ask if treatment can be done without pills. The honest answer is: sometimes yes, sometimes no. It depends on the problem, the intensity of symptoms and the risks.

Some patients improve with physiotherapy, procedures, exercises and simple routine changes. Others need medication, at least for a period of time. That is not a failure. The important thing is that treatment is suitable and monitored.

What if side effects appear?

It is normal to ask this. Sometimes a treatment needs adjustment. Sometimes the body needs a few days to adapt. Sometimes the medication needs to be changed.

The goal is not to tolerate something that does not suit you. The goal is to find, together with the doctor, an option that helps and causes as little discomfort as possible.

With stress, sleep problems, nervous tension or chronic pain, change does not always feel instant. The nervous system needs time. That is why it matters to tell the doctor how you feel after starting treatment.

Why Revimed PLUS+?

We do not promise a magic solution for every case. We try to find a logical solution, explain it clearly and adapt it to the person in front of us.

You can choose classic medical treatment or, if the situation allows it, gentler methods. Everything is done with the patient’s agreement and based on what is safe and realistic for the case.
