Etrans-2

Device for transcranial electroanalgesia procedures.

Description

  • Etrans-2 belongs to the group of devices used for transcranial electroanalgesia and electrotranquilization.
  • The procedure is performed with frontal-mastoid or frontal-occipital electrode placement, depending on the protocol.
  • Duration and parameters are selected individually.

Important

  • The method is not used independently without medical evaluation.
  • Contraindications must be excluded before treatment.

⚠️ The information is educational and does not replace medical consultation.
