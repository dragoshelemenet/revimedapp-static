AMP Device

Spectral blood formula analyzer / express laboratory for fast, non-invasive orientation.

What it may assess

  • blood formula and selected metabolic parameters;
  • electrolyte balance and neuromuscular conductivity;
  • blood flow and oxygen consumption in tissues;
  • functional compensation capacity of the body;
  • orientation markers for inflammatory or functional imbalance.

Important medical note

  • The result should not be interpreted as an automatic diagnosis.
  • The physician interprets the findings together with symptoms, history, examination and, when needed, standard laboratory or imaging tests.
  • The device can be useful as a fast functional screening tool, but it does not replace full medical diagnosis.

Express diagnostics in Chisinau: AMP device for fast medical orientation

Pe scurt: The AMP device is used for express diagnostics and fast orientative evaluation. At Revimed PLUS+ Chisinau, it does not replace classical laboratory tests, but it can help the doctor understand the possible direction of a problem faster.

This type of evaluation may be useful when a patient has several unclear symptoms: fatigue, dizziness, headaches, nervous tension, poor sleep, weakness or slow recovery.

What is the AMP device?

The AMP device belongs to the category of express diagnostic equipment. In practice, it is used for a fast, non-invasive and orientative evaluation.

The patient should not understand it as a classical laboratory blood test, but as an additional method that gives the doctor initial information for the clinical discussion.

Why can express diagnostics be useful?

In Chisinau and Moldova, many people look for a quick evaluation because they do not know where to start: neurologist, therapist, tests, physiotherapy, recovery or stress-related treatment.

Express diagnostics can be useful as a first step for structuring the consultation and choosing a clearer direction.

What can it show and what should not be promised?

An express diagnostic device can provide clues and orientative directions, but it should not be presented as a device that sees all diseases.

It does not replace blood tests, imaging investigations or specialist consultations. If symptoms are severe or sudden, the patient needs a complete medical evaluation.

Who may benefit from this evaluation?

People with persistent fatigue, dizziness, nervous tension, poor sleep, weakness or slow recovery.

Patients who have several small but repeated symptoms and want a starting medical direction in Chisinau.

Why does the doctor’s interpretation matter?

An isolated result is not enough. Symptoms, age, stress, sleep, nutrition, medical history, medications and previous treatment reactions all matter.

At Revimed PLUS+, the result of an express evaluation is discussed with the doctor. The doctor can decide whether further tests, physiotherapy, neurological consultation, recovery procedures or medication are needed.

The Revimed PLUS+ approach

Revimed PLUS+ is a clinic founded in 2005 in Chisinau, Moldova. Our doctor has over 35 years of experience, and treatment is selected individually for each patient.

The goal is for the patient to understand what can be evaluated, what the method’s limits are and what the next steps may be.
