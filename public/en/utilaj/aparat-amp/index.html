Express diagnostics in Chisinau: AMP device for fast medical orientation

The AMP device is used for express diagnostics and fast orientative evaluation. At Revimed PLUS+ Chisinau, it does not replace classical laboratory tests, but it can help the doctor understand the possible direction of a problem faster.

What is the AMP device?

The AMP device belongs to the category of express diagnostic equipment. In practice, it is used for fast, non-invasive and orientative evaluation. It is not a classical laboratory blood test, but an additional orientation method.

When can it be useful?

It may be useful when a patient has unclear symptoms: fatigue, dizziness, headaches, nervous tension, poor sleep, weakness or slow recovery.

What should not be promised?

The device can provide orientative clues, but it does not see all diseases and does not replace blood tests, imaging investigations or specialist consultations.

Why does the doctor matter?

The result is interpreted together with symptoms, patient history, stress, sleep, previous treatments and general condition. At Revimed PLUS+, decisions are made individually, with the patient’s agreement.
